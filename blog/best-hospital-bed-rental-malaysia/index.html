What to Look for in a Hospital Bed Rental Service

When choosing a hospital bed rental provider in Malaysia, consider these important factors:

  • KKM Approval - Certified by Malaysia's Ministry of Health

Types of Hospital Beds Available for Rent

2-Function Manual Hospital Bed

The most popular choice for home care. Allows adjustment of:

  • Head section - Raise for sitting, eating, watching TV
  • Leg section - Elevate for better circulation

Best for: Post-surgery recovery, elderly care, patients who can still move somewhat independently

Price: RM150/month rental | RM799 to buy

3-Function Manual Hospital Bed

Adds one crucial feature over the 2-function bed:

  • Head section adjustment
  • Leg section adjustment
  • Height adjustment - Raise/lower the entire bed

Best for: Bedridden patients, caregivers with back problems, long-term care

Price: RM200-250/month rental | RM1,299 to buy

Expert Tip: The height adjustment feature is primarily for the caregiver, not the patient. If you're caring for someone alone and need to change diapers, give bed baths, or do physiotherapy, the 3-function bed will save your back.

Electric Hospital Bed (6-Function)

Motorized bed with push-button controls. Features include:

  • Electric head adjustment
  • Electric leg adjustment
  • Electric height adjustment
  • Trendelenburg position
  • Reverse Trendelenburg
  • CPR quick-release

Best for: Long-term care, patients with limited mobility, facilities

Price: RM300-350/month rental | RM3,500+ to buy
