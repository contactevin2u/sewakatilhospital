Benefits of Hospital Bed at Home

Home care with the right hospital bed provides safety, comfort and convenience for patients and caregivers.

Avoid Hospital Infections (HAI)

21.6% of hospital patients in Southeast Asia contract HAI. Home care reduces exposure to hospital germs.

Prevent Pressure Sores (Bedsore)

1 in 7 hospital patients experience pressure sores. Ripple mattress prevents skin damage through alternating pressure.

Side Rails Prevent Falls

Safety rails reduce fall risk by up to 55%. Lockable wheels keep the bed stable.

Protect Caregiver Health

Adjustable bed height prevents caregiver back pain. No need to bend over when caring for patients.

Safer Eating

Elevate head 30-45° to reduce choking risk (aspiration). Safe position for eating & drinking.

Easy Care & Clean

Adjustable surface makes diaper changes, bathing & wound care easier. ABS material is easy to clean.

FAQ About Hospital Bed Rental Malaysia

Get instant answers - no need to wait. Or WhatsApp us for further assistance.

How much is the monthly hospital bed rental?

Hospital bed rental starts from RM150 per month for a 2 function hospital bed (manual). For 3 function hospital beds and electric beds, prices vary by model. Contact us for the latest quotation.

What's the difference between 2 function and 3 function hospital beds?

2 Function Hospital Bed: Can adjust head section (up/down) and leg section (up/down). Suitable for patients who need semi-reclined rest or leg elevation.

3 Function Hospital Bed: Same as 2 function, plus overall bed height adjustment. Makes it easier for caregivers to attend to patients without bending over. More suitable for fully bedridden patients.

Do you have electric hospital beds?

Yes, we have electric hospital beds with remote control. No need to turn manual cranks - just press a button to adjust head, legs and height. Very suitable for elderly or caregivers who find it difficult to turn cranks. Contact us for model options and pricing.

How does installation and delivery work?

FREE Installation - Our team will come and install the bed at your home at no extra charge. We will also teach you how to use it properly.

Delivery - Delivery service is available throughout Malaysia. Contact us for booking.

Can I pay by instalment?

Yes! We understand that medical expenses can be burdensome. We accept instalment payments for hospital bed purchases. Contact us to discuss a payment plan that suits your budget.

Can I sell the hospital bed back to you?

Yes, we have a trade-in program. If you bought a hospital bed from us and no longer need it, you can sell it back to us. *Subject to terms and conditions.
